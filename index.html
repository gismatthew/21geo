About Us

21st Century Geomatics (21st Geo) has been providing remote sensing data and GIS-customized services to industry clients in western Canada. 21st Geo is a privately owned Canadian corporation in business since 2012. Working primarily with up-to-date satellite imagery, we provide expert advice, appropriate data, and program solutions for terrestrial mapping and monitoring in renewable and non-renewable resource sectors. Utilizing advanced optimization algorithms, we help our users to optimize business resource planning to do more business with less resources.

Services

GIS Service

21st Century Geomatics specializes in creating rich and easy-to-use mapping applications for companies, government agencies, and non-profit organizations of all sizes. 21st Century Geomatics delivers software solutions that allow clients to efficiently accomplish tasks with increased precision in their workflow, while at the same time reducing time and cost. Complementing this development expertise, our team has over 20 years’ experience in digital mapping and database management, ensuring an in-depth knowledge of the relevant technical issues that is second to none. Our goal, through custom GIS application development, is to provide our clients with the tools they need to not only improve their business, but also enhance the end-users experience by providing the spatial information necessary to quickly make well-informed decisions.

GPS Service

The key of any GIS system is the spatial data upon which the display and query operations rely. Acquisition of this data can be completed using a variety of different sources and methodologies. 21st Century Geomatics maintains state-of-the-art GPS data collection equipment capable of sub-meter accuracy survey needs. We had ArcGIS, QGIS, PCI, AutoCAD software and Geodatabase maintenance expertise allowing us to utilize advance digitizing techniques in essentially any environment using any map data format.

Products

Scheduling

Every organization faces scheduling puzzles: assign a limited set of constrained resources (employees, assets, time and money) to provide products or services to customers. To solve this problem, 21st Century Geomatics has developed a spatial scheduling prototype system OptiSchedule. OptiSchedule features:

  • Integrate attributive constraints and Spatial constraints to optimize Employee scheduling
  • Integrate employee information management, constraints management into one platform
  • Combines sophisticated optimization heuristics and metaheuristic with very efficient score calculation
  • Integrate Google Mapping to display best route results

If you need demo or more information, please contact mma@21geo.com.

GIS

21st Century Geomatics has developed the custom on-line Google Mapping Applications RentHelper.ca and NiceSask.ca.

21st Century Geomatics developed an initial Google Mapping application for RentHelper approximately 2 years ago. Over the past 2 years, RentHelper and 21st Century Geomatics have continued to enhance the mapping functionality making it one of the most functional Google Mapping applications in the industry. Users now have the ability to view supplemental layers, query for specific listings matching their criteria, as well as view amenities near a particular listing.

21st Century Geomatics is currently working with RentHelper and several other partners in developing mobile mapping applications for the iOs and Blackberry devices.

21st Century Geomatics has also developed a custom Google Mapping application for NiceSask.ca. NiceSask is a web-based mapping information hub of Saskatoon City. It listed public sector, school, restaurant, hospital & clinic, travel center, shopping center, house maintenance etc. to serve local community people.
