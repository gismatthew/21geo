About Us

21st Century Geomatics (21st Geo) has been providing remote sensing data and GIS-customized services to industry clients in western Canada. 21st Geo is a privately owned Canadian corporation in business since 2012. Working primarily with up-to-date satellite imagery, we provide expert advice, appropriate data, and program solutions for terrestrial mapping and monitoring in renewable and non-renewable resource sectors. Utilizing advanced optimization algorithms, we help our users to optimize business resource planning to do more business with less resources.

Services

GIS Service

Utilizing our own 21st WebGIS Platform, 21st Century Geomatics specializes in creating rich and easy-to-use mapping applications for companies, government agencies with increased precision in their workflow, while at the same time reducing time and cost.

RS Service

21st Century Geomatics is an experienced provider of remote sensing and geospatial services. The company specialized in data acquisition and integration, satellite imagery processing and analysis, change detection and features extraction.

GPS Service

The key of any GIS system is the spatial data upon which the display and query operations rely. 21st Century Geomatics maintains state-of-the-art GPS data collection equipment capable of sub-meter accuracy survey to meet users' all needs.
